Related Diseases

Common diseases: Hypertension (high blood pressure) strains heart, caused by stress, diet. Atherosclerosis: Plaque buildup narrows arteries, leading to heart attacks or strokes. Heart failure: Heart can't pump enough. Anemia: Low RBCs, fatigue. Leukemia: Cancer of WBCs. Hemophilia: Clotting disorder. Varicose veins: Swollen veins. Prevention: Balanced diet, exercise, no smoking. Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness breath. Treatments: Medications, surgery like bypass. Global: CVDs top killer. In kids, congenital defects like holes in heart. Vaccines prevent rheumatic heart disease. Awareness campaigns help. (Word count: ~505)
