Fun Facts

Your heart beats 2.5 billion times in a lifetime. Blood is red from iron in hemoglobin; blue in veins due to light. Octopuses have blue blood from copper. Giraffes have high pressure to reach heads. No blood type in insects. Heart can beat outside body briefly. Sneezing stops heart momentarily. Laughter improves flow. Coconut water like plasma. Women faster heartbeat. Space weakens heart.
